OpenAccountants is AGPL-3.0. AGPL-3.0 projects below are same-license family — fully compatible.

## openfisca-aotearoa

- Repository: [BetterRules/openfisca-aotearoa](https://github.com/BetterRules/openfisca-aotearoa)
- License: AGPL-3.0
- Stars: 41
- Contributors: 15
- Language: Python
- Scope: Aotearoa New Zealand legislation encoded as computable rules using the OpenFisca framework. Covers income tax brackets, Working for Families (WFF) tax credits, Best Start payments, accommodation supplement, community services card eligibility, jobseeker support, sole parent support, student allowance, and various social welfare entitlements.
- Why it matters: Government-supported "Better Rules" initiative to make legislation machine-readable. 41 stars and 15 contributors including government/academic participants. Same AGPL-3.0 license as OpenAccountants.
- Integration approach: NZ income tax bracket definitions, WFF credit calculations, and social welfare entitlement rules directly referenceable. Strong validation source for NZ tax rates and thresholds.
