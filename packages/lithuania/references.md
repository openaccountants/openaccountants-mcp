## income-tax-calculator

- Repository: [sarunas/income-tax-calculator](https://github.com/sarunas/income-tax-calculator)
- License: MIT
- Stars: 21
- Language: TypeScript
- Scope: Lithuanian tax tool that converts IBI (investment brokerage) reports to VMI (Lithuanian tax authority) tax declaration format. Handles capital gains and investment income reporting for Lithuanian taxpayers.
- Integration: MIT. Freely usable with attribution. IBI-to-VMI conversion logic and declaration format mappings can be adapted.
